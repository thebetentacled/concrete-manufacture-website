Green Cement

In New Jersey, a company named Solida is finding a good use for the Carbon Dioxide that results from the manufacturing process (Ramsden 7). The carbon is captured and used in a mixture to fill up the empty spaces in the recently made cement, as well as being used to cure the cement. Carbon Sequestration

Carbon Sequestration, a technology that captures Carbon Dioxide present in the atmosphere is an expensive but effective solution for reducing the impact of all manner of industries. However, it is still impractical for most plants due to its steep cost, running more than $600 per ton of CO2 (Ramsden 8).
